The 12 Agile Principles

  1. Satisfy the customer through early and continuous delivery of valuable software
  2. Changing requirements is welcome, even late in development
  3. Deliver working software frequently, with a preference to the shorter timescale.
  4. The devlopers' and business' side must work together on a daily basis
  5. Projects are built around motivated individuals with trust.
  6. Face-to-face conversation is the best form of communication in a dev team
  7. Sustainable development where constant pace is maintained stands.
  8. Software that works in the best indicator for progress
  9. Attention to detail, excellence and good design improves performance
  10. Simplicity is essential
  11. The best happens in self-organizing teams
  12. The team reflects on how to improve regularly and adapts
